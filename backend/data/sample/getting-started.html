Getting Started with Nexus

This guide walks you through setting up your Nexus workspace and inviting your team.

Step 1: Create your workspace

Sign up at nexus.example/signup with your work email. A workspace is automatically created for you. The workspace name is visible to your teammates, so use your company or team name.

Step 2: Invite team members

Go to Settings → Members and enter your teammates' email addresses. You can assign roles: Owner, Admin, Member, or Guest. Guests can view and comment but cannot create or edit tasks.

Step 3: Create your first project

Click "New Project" in the left sidebar. Choose a template (Software, Marketing, Operations, or blank) or start from scratch. Every project gets its own board, list, and timeline view.

Step 4: Add tasks

Inside a project, click "Add task" or press N. Give the task a title, assign it to a team member, set a due date, and add a priority (Urgent, High, Normal, or Low). Tasks can have sub-tasks, attachments, and comments.

Step 5: Connect your tools

Go to Settings → Integrations to connect GitHub, Slack, Google Calendar, or other apps. The GitHub integration automatically links pull requests and commits to tasks.

Keyboard shortcuts

Press ? anywhere in the app to open the shortcuts panel. Common shortcuts: N to create a task, / to search, G then P to go to Projects, and Ctrl+K (Cmd+K on Mac) for the command palette.
