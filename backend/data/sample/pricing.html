Nexus Pricing Plans

Nexus offers three plans. All plans include unlimited projects and a 14-day free trial — no credit card required.

Free plan

Ideal for individuals and small teams just getting started. Includes: up to 5 members, 3 active projects, 1 GB file storage, and basic board and list views. Community support only.

Pro plan — $12 per member per month

For growing teams that need more power. Everything in Free, plus: unlimited members and projects, timeline (Gantt) view, time tracking, automations (1,000 runs/month), priority support, and 100 GB file storage. Billed annually or $15 month-to-month.

Enterprise plan — custom pricing

For large organisations with advanced security and compliance needs. Everything in Pro, plus: SSO/SAML, audit logs, custom data retention, SLA-backed 99.99% uptime, dedicated customer success manager, unlimited automation runs, and 1 TB file storage. Contact sales@nexus.example for a quote.

Education and non-profit discounts

Verified educational institutions and registered non-profits receive 50% off the Pro plan. Apply at nexus.example/discounts with proof of eligibility.

Billing and payment

Accepted payment methods: Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and PayPal. Invoices are emailed automatically. Annual subscribers can request a PDF invoice for accounting purposes. Refunds are available within 30 days of purchase if you are not satisfied.

Upgrading and downgrading

You can upgrade your plan at any time; the charge is prorated for the remainder of your billing cycle. Downgrading takes effect at the end of the current billing period. If you exceed the Free plan's project limit after downgrading, existing projects become read-only until you reduce the active count.
