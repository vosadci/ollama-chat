Bank Accounts

DemoBank offers a range of personal and business accounts to suit your needs.

Current Account

Our standard current account is free to open and maintain. It includes a debit card, online banking access, and up to 50 free transactions per month. Overdraft facilities are available on request.

Savings Account

Earn interest on your balance with our flexible savings account. No minimum deposit is required. The current annual interest rate is 2.5%. Funds can be withdrawn at any time without penalty.

Term Deposit

Lock in a higher rate with a fixed-term deposit. Terms range from 3 to 24 months. Interest rates up to 4.8% per annum depending on the term. Early withdrawal is subject to a fee.

Business Account

Designed for sole traders and small businesses. Includes dedicated IBAN, multi-user access, and monthly statements. First 6 months are fee-free.

How to Open an Account

You can open any account online in under 10 minutes. You will need a valid government-issued ID and proof of address. Alternatively, visit any DemoBank branch with your documents.
