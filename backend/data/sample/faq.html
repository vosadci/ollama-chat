Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot password" on the login page and enter your email address. You will receive a reset link within a few minutes. If it does not arrive, check your spam folder or contact support@nexus.example.

Can I use Nexus offline?

The mobile apps (iOS and Android) support offline mode. Changes you make offline sync automatically when your device reconnects. The web app requires an internet connection.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to Settings → Billing and click "Cancel subscription". Your plan remains active until the end of the current billing period, after which it reverts to the Free plan. Your data is retained for 90 days after cancellation.

Is my data secure?

Yes. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). Nexus is SOC 2 Type II certified and GDPR compliant. Backups are taken every 6 hours and retained for 30 days. You can export all your data at any time from Settings → Export.

How many members can I invite?

The Free plan supports up to 5 members. The Pro and Enterprise plans have unlimited members. Guest users (view and comment only) do not count toward your member limit on any plan.

What happens if I exceed my storage limit?

You will receive an email warning at 80% and 95% of your storage limit. If you reach 100%, new file uploads are blocked but existing files remain accessible. Upgrade your plan or delete unused files to free up space.

Does Nexus have an API?

Yes. The Nexus REST API and webhooks are available on Pro and Enterprise plans. API documentation is at developers.nexus.example. Rate limits are 1,000 requests per minute per workspace.

How do I import data from another tool?

Nexus can import from Jira, Trello, Asana, and CSV files. Go to Settings → Import and select your source. The import wizard maps fields automatically and flags any conflicts for you to resolve manually.
