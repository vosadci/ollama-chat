Frequently Asked Questions

How do I open an account?

You can open an account online at demobank.example/open-account or visit any branch. You need a valid government-issued ID and proof of address. The process takes about 10 minutes online.

What are the fees for a current account?

The standard current account has no monthly maintenance fee. Fees apply only for specific services such as international wire transfers (€5 per transfer) or paper statements (€2 per month).

How do I reset my online banking password?

Click "Forgot password" on the login page. You will receive a reset link to your registered email address. If you do not receive it within 5 minutes, check your spam folder or call customer support.

What is the daily spending limit on my debit card?

The default daily limit is €2,000 for purchases and €500 for ATM withdrawals. You can adjust these limits temporarily or permanently in the mobile app under Card Settings.

How do I dispute a transaction?

If you notice an unauthorized or incorrect transaction, report it within 30 days via the mobile app (select the transaction and tap "Dispute") or call 0800 123 456. DemoBank will investigate and provide a resolution within 10 business days.

Is my money protected?

Yes. Deposits at DemoBank are protected up to €100,000 per person under the national deposit guarantee scheme.

How do I close my account?

To close your account, visit a branch with a valid ID or submit a written request by post. Ensure your balance is zero and all direct debits are cancelled before closing.
