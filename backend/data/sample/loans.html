Loans and Financing

DemoBank offers competitive personal loans and mortgage products with transparent terms.

Personal Loan

Borrow between €1,000 and €50,000 for any purpose — home improvements, a car, education, or consolidating existing debt. Repayment terms from 12 to 84 months. Annual Percentage Rate (APR) from 7.9% depending on the amount and your credit profile. No early repayment fee.

Mortgage

Finance your home purchase with a DemoBank mortgage. Loan-to-value up to 80%. Fixed-rate options for 5, 10, or 15 years. Variable-rate mortgages also available. A minimum down payment of 20% is required. The application process includes a property valuation.

Overdraft

An arranged overdraft is available on current accounts up to €2,000. Interest charged only on the amount used, at a rate of 14.9% EAR. No monthly fee for having the facility in place.

How to Apply for a Loan

You can apply for a personal loan online in minutes. Required documents: proof of income (last 3 payslips or tax return), a valid ID, and your DemoBank account details. A credit decision is typically provided within one business day. Mortgage applications require an in-branch appointment.

Responsible Lending

DemoBank assesses all applications responsibly. Borrowing should suit your financial situation. Contact our advisors if you need help understanding your options.
