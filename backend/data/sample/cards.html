Debit and Credit Cards

DemoBank issues Visa and Mastercard cards accepted worldwide.

Standard Debit Card

Issued free with every current account. Supports contactless payments, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. Daily ATM withdrawal limit is €500. No foreign transaction fee within the EU.

Classic Credit Card

Credit limit from €500 to €5,000 based on your credit assessment. Interest-free period of up to 45 days. Annual fee: €24. Minimum monthly repayment: 5% of outstanding balance.

Gold Credit Card

For customers with a strong credit history. Credit limit up to €15,000. Cashback of 1% on all purchases. Travel insurance included. Annual fee: €60. Interest-free period of up to 55 days.

Virtual Card

A virtual card for online purchases only, linked to your current account. Issued instantly. Supports 3D Secure authentication.

Card Security

All cards use chip-and-PIN technology. You can freeze or unfreeze your card instantly via the mobile app. Report a lost or stolen card by calling 0800 123 789 at any time.

Applying for a Credit Card

Apply online or in branch. You must be at least 18 years old and a DemoBank current account holder for at least 3 months. Approval is subject to a credit check.
