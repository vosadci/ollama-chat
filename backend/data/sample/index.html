Welcome to Nexus

Nexus is a cloud-based project management and team collaboration platform. It helps teams plan work, track progress, and ship faster — all in one place.

Nexus is used by over 10,000 teams worldwide, from independent freelancers to enterprises with hundreds of members. It integrates with the tools you already use, including GitHub, Slack, Figma, and Google Workspace.

Core capabilities

  • Task and project management with boards, lists, and timelines
  • Real-time collaboration: comments, mentions, and live editing
  • Time tracking and workload management
  • Automated workflows and recurring tasks
  • Reporting and analytics dashboards
  • Native mobile apps for iOS and Android

Getting help

Support is available via live chat inside the app (Monday–Friday 09:00–18:00 UTC) or by email at support@nexus.example. Enterprise customers have access to a dedicated success manager and 24/7 priority support.
